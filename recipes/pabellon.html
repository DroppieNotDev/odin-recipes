Ingredients

  • -Precooked corn flour (e.g., Harina P.A.N.)
  • - Warm water
  • - Salt
  • -Oil (for cooking)
  • -Cheese
  • -Shredded Beef or chicken
  • -Black Beans
  • -Platain

Step by Step

  1. Meassure the Ingredients
  2. Mix the Dough
  3. Knead and Rest
  4. Shape the Arepas
  5. Coon on Skillet
  6. Bake for Fluffiness(Optional)
  7. Slice and Fill
