La Catira

Description

"La Catira" (meaning "the blonde") is the chicken version of "La Pelua." It's a delicious mix of shredded chicken and yellow cheese.

Ingredients

  • -Precooked corn flour (e.g., Harina P.A.N.)
  • - Warm water
  • - Salt
  • -Oil (for cooking)
  • -Yellow Cheese
  • -Shredded chicken

Step by Step

  1. Meassure the Ingredients
  2. Mix the Dough
  3. Knead and Rest
  4. Shape the Arepas
  5. Coon on Skillet
  6. Bake for Fluffiness(Optional)
  7. Slice and Fill
