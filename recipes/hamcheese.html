Jamon y Queso

Description

The Venezuelan version of a classic ham and cheese sandwich, but in a warm, crispy arepa. It's simple, cheesy, and comforting.

Ingredients

  • -Precooked corn flour (e.g., Harina P.A.N.)
  • - Warm water
  • - Salt
  • -Oil (for cooking)
  • -Shredded Yellow Cheese
  • -Ham

Step by Step

  1. Meassure the Ingredients
  2. Mix the Dough
  3. Knead and Rest
  4. Shape the Arepas
  5. Coon on Skillet
  6. Bake for Fluffiness(Optional)
  7. Slice and Fill
