Description

Las arepas llaneras are a classic Venezuelan style of arepa that originates from the "Llano" plains. They are famous for being stuffed with a hearty and flavorful stew called "carne mechada" (shredded beef cooked with tomatoes, onions, and peppers).

    Ingredients

  • -Precooked corn flour (e.g., Harina P.A.N.)
  • - Warm water
  • - Salt
  • -Oil (for cooking)
  • -Cheese(Queso de Mano)
  • -Shredded Beef or chicken
  • -Tomatoes
  • -Avocado

    Step by Step

  1. 1.Meassure the Ingredients
  2. 2.Mix the Dough
  3. 3.Knead and Rest
  4. 4.Shape the Arepas
  5. 5.Coon on Skillet
  6. 6.Bake for Fluffiness(Optional)
  7. 7.Slice and Fill
