Domino

Description

Named for its resemblance to domino pieces, this is the simplest arepa: black beans and white cheese. It's a classic, meat-free option.

Ingredients

  • -Precooked corn flour (e.g., Harina P.A.N.)
  • - Warm water
  • - Salt
  • -Oil (for cooking)
  • -White Cheese
  • -Black Beans

Step by Step

  1. Meassure the Ingredients
  2. Mix the Dough
  3. Knead and Rest
  4. Shape the Arepas
  5. Coon on Skillet
  6. Bake for Fluffiness(Optional)
  7. Slice and Fill
